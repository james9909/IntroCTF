IntroCTF

New platform!

We've migrated to a brand new platform, using Flask for the framework, and SQLite3 to handle the data. As a result, the platform will give a faster, more fluid experience for users, compared to our older platform. Most importantly, the new platform is extensible, making it easier to add new features.

Problems have been taken down, but if you want, contact one of us and we'll put them up again.

For the full list of changes, view the changelog.

As always, feel free to view the source on github.

Changelog

  1. Migrated from cgi to Flask.
  2. Migrated from csv databases to SQLite3 databases.
  3. Increased security and cleaned up codebase.
  4. Unified teams under one account.
  5. Changed sorting of problems to point value, not category.
  6. Added an admin interface with the following features:
    • - Easy method for adding adding/updating/removing problems.
    • - Ability to preview problems.
    • - Export data to json.
  7. Added a graph for the score progression of the top 5 teams to the scoreboard.
