How to play:
  1. Register an individual user account, and either make your own team or join a friend's. Note that team are limited to 3 members
  2. Log into your user account and solve problems in the problems page.
  3. View the scoreboard to see how your team is doing against other teams.
  4. Click a team on the scoreboard to find out more information about them.
  5. If you are done, make sure to click the logout button in the top right.
  6. If you have any questions, feel free to message one of us or something
  7. also pls dont cheat
